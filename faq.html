FAQ

Q: Where did you get the idea for Simpsons quotes?

A: Our team all had a similar interest in common (watching The Simpsons) and wanted to share our interest and passion with others that would appreciate it. Besides, with how funny the characters are, how could they not sell?

Q: Are all of these quotes really from the show?

A: Yep, every single one is something someone has said in a Simpsons episode.

Q: Do the shirts you sell go in any sort of order with the episodes?

A: No, the shirts have no order correspondence with the episodes. We just pick our favorite quotes throughout all the seasons and are always thinking of more.

Q: Will you ever make anything other than t-shirts?

A: As of right now, our main focus is the t-shirts. However, there are ideas in the works for other apparel and merchandise that will be announced when complete.

Q: What other ideas are in the making?

A: Nothing is set in stone, but we were thinking perhaps phone cases, hats, sweatshirts, and socks (to name a few).

Q: Will you ever do any other shows?

A: Right now, our main focus is to further establish the D'ohments brand. However, later down the road there is a possibility of expanding our work to other lines.
